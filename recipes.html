Profile

"recipe medication" refers to a formulated herbal mixture prepared using various parts of plants (like roots, leaves, flowers, seeds) combined with specific ratios and processes, often including decoctions (kashayam), infusions, powders, oils, or pastes, designed to target specific health concerns based on an individual's "dosha" balance (vata, pitta, kapha) and tailored by an Ayurvedic practitioner.
