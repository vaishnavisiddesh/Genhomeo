How to stay hydrated
  • Drink plenty of fluids like water, diluted fruit juice, and tea
  • Eat foods with high water content, like fruits, vegetables, and greens
  • Drink regularly throughout the day
  • Increase your fluid intake if you're active or it's hot
  • Replace fluids if you're sick with a fever, vomiting, or diarrhea
How to tell if you're hydrated

  • Check the color of your urine
  • Pale yellow or straw-colored urine indicates good hydration
  • Darker yellow urine indicates a need for more water

    • Helps the digestive system function
    • Keeps skin healthy
    • Helps regulate body temperature
    • Helps keep concentration up
    • Improves sleep quality, cognition, and mood
    • Helps prevent infections
    • Delivers nutrients to cells
