IYENGAR YOGA


Benifits of Iyengar yoga

  • Improves Posture: The focus on alignment helps correct posture and body mechanics.

  • Enhances Flexibility and Strength: Long-held poses build strength while improving flexibility in muscles and joints.

  • Improves Focus and Concentration: The attention to detail helps quiet the mind and encourages mindfulness.
  • Reduces Stress and Anxiety: The practice promotes relaxation and calm through controlled breathing and balanced poses.

  • Encourages Emotional Stability: Holding poses helps release tension and fosters emotional resilience.
  • Alleviates Chronic Pain: Targeted alignment can help relieve back, neck, and joint pain.

  • Improves Circulation: Stimulates blood flow and oxygenation throughout the body, promoting overall health.

  • Supports Digestive Health: Certain poses aid in stimulating digestion and improving gut health.
