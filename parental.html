PREGNANCY YOGA


Benifits of Pregnancy yoga

  • Reduces Aches and Pains: Helps alleviate common pregnancy discomforts like back pain, swollen ankles, and hip tightness.

  • Enhances Flexibility and Strength: Prepares the body for the physical demands of labor and delivery by strengthening key muscle groups.

  • Supports Joint Health: Gentle, intentional movements reduce tension and improve joint mobility.
  • Improves Posture: Gentle stretching and alignment exercises counteract posture changes caused by the growing baby bump.

  • Supports Breathing Techniques: Pranayama (controlled breathing) improves oxygen flow, benefiting both mother and baby.
  • Reduces Stress and Anxiety: Relaxation techniques help calm the mind and ease emotional turbulence.

  • Boosts Emotional Resilience: Encourages mindfulness, patience, and a sense of connection with the baby.

  • Improves Sleep Quality: Yoga promotes relaxation, which can help combat pregnancy-related insomnia.
