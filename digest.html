DIGESTIVE BOOSTING RECIPES

Digestive-boosting recipes are simple and effective ways to support gut health using natural ingredients. One popular remedy is herbal ginger tea, made by steeping freshly grated ginger in hot water with lemon and honey. Ginger stimulates gastric enzymes, while lemon provides vitamin C, and honey soothes the stomach. For a probiotic-rich option, a yogurt smoothie with banana, ground flaxseed, and honey works wonders. Yogurt introduces healthy bacteria, while flaxseed adds dietary fiber, promoting smooth digestion.
